Instructions
This is a single-player game where you battle against the computer.

1) You each receive a random Pokemon card. The player card will have 4 attributes: HP, Attack, Defense, Speed.

2) Choose an attribute to play with by clicking on it.

3) Once an attribute is chosen, the computers card is revealed and the chosen attribute on each card is compared.

4) The player with the higher attribute score wins the round and earns 1 point. In case of a draw, the player with the highest total of all the attributes on their card wins the round.

5) You will play 5 rounds in total and can choose a new attribute to play with each round.

6) At the end of the 5 rounds, the player with the most rounds won is the overall winner of the game.

Enjoy your game of Pokemon Top Trumps!
